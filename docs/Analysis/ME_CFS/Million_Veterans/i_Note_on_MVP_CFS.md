# Note on Phenotype

I analyzed the Million Veterans Program GWAS of chronic fatigue syndrome (CFS).   Note that Million Veterans GWAS are based on PheCodes, which are aggregations of billing codes.  [As observed on the Science for ME forum](https://s4me.info/threads/million-veterans-program-mvp-data-on-me-cfs.50532/post-697541) this Million Veterans GWAS uses PheCode 798.1, which aggregates Chronic fatigue syndrome, Post-viral fatigue syndrome, and Chronic fatigue unspecified.  It is reasonable to expect that both the aggregation of multiple distinct billing codes (i.e. aggregation of unspecified fatigue with chronic fatigue syndrome) and imprecise use of these billing codes could add considerable noise to this GWAS.
