# S-LDSC Analysis

I applied [Stratified Linkage Disequilibrium Score Regression](../../../Bioinformatics_Concepts/S_LDSC_For_Cell_And_Tissue_ID.md)[@finucane2018heritability] (S-LDSC) to Kamitaki et al.'s GWAS of Human Herpesvirus 7 DNA levels[@kamitaki2025genes]. There were no significant cell types in any of the datasets.  It is interesting to contrast this the [corresponding analysis for EBV DNA levels](../../Epstein_Barr/Nyeo_et_al_2025/S-LDSC-EBV.md), which produced several significant cells types.  This may indicate an overall weaker signal in the HHV7 GWAS.
