# S-LDSC


I applied [Stratified Linkage Disequilibrium Score Regression](../../../Bioinformatics_Concepts/S_LDSC_For_Cell_And_Tissue_ID.md)[@finucane2018heritability] (S-LDSC) to summary statistics from Said et al.'s GWAS of CRP[@said2022genetic].  Surprisingly, and in contrast to the [MAGMA results](MAGMA_CRP_Analysis.md), there were no significant hits with any of the standard S-LDSC reference datasets.
