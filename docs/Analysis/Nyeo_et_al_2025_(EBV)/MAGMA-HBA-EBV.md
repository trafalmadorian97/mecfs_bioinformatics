# MAGMA HBA Analysis
I applied [MAGMA](../../Bioinformatics_Concepts/MAGMA_Overview.md)[@de2015magma] to Nyeo et al.'s GWAS of Epstein-Barr Virus (EBV) DNA[@nyeo2026population] using scRNAseq data from the [Human Brain Atlas](../../Data_Sources/HBA_scRNAseq.md)[@siletti2023transcriptomic] (HBA) as a reference.

Since an overwhelming majority of people are infected by EBV, the level of EBV DNA in a person's blood is primarily a function of how well that person's adaptive immune system is able to keep their infection under control and in a dormant state. It thus makes sense that MAGMA analysis should point to [T lymphocytes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/T_cell), which are central cells of the adaptive immune system, as key determinants of EBV DNA levels.
