# LDSC

I applied [Linkage Disequilibrium Score Regression](../../Bioinformatics_Concepts/LDSC.md)[@bulik2015ld] to the triglyceride GWAS of Willer et al.[@global2013discovery].  The aim was to estimate heritability and look for signs of stratification and confounding.


The results are below:

The LDSC intercept of 1.014 suggests minimal evidence for population stratification.

The SNP heritability of 0.1958 is low-to-moderate.
