
# LDSC

[Linkage Disequilibrium Score Regression](../../../Bioinformatics_Concepts/LDSC.md)[@bulik2015ld] was applied to the UK BioBank GWAS of migraine[@uk2025whole][@GCST90473326_migraine].  The aim was to estimate heritability and look for signs of stratification and confounding.

The results are below:


{{ include_file("docs/_figs/uk_biobank_2025_migraine_eur_ldsc_heritability_markdown.mdx") }}


The heritability of 0.09394 in this UK BioBank study is comparable to the heritability calculated from a migraine GWAS of a different cohort from [Verma et al.](../Verma_et_al/Migraine_LDSC.md)[@verma2024diversity], where the heritability was 0.1005.

The attenuation ratio of 0.2108 in this study indicates less potential confounding from population stratification than that calculated from the Verma et al. study[@verma2024diversity], where the ratio was 0.3561.
